About Me

With a background in software development and a passion for helping others, I've transitioned my skills to excel as a Technical Support Specialist at OneUp, and later as a Customer Enablement Specialist. I'm known for my strong work ethic, positive attitude, and ability to juggle multiple priorities. I'm dedicated to providing excellent service, eager to learn new technologies, and always willing to go the extra mile to contribute to the team's success.

Customer Enablement Specialist

At OneUp, I focus on ensuring customers achieve maximum success and value from our platform. I blend strategic one-to-one guidance for key accounts with creating scalable, automated journeys that benefit our entire user base. I'm passionate about proactive problem-solving, exemplified by creating a workflow that identifies underutilization and delivers automated, targeted advice. As the Customer Success Manager for 70% of our clients, I handle everything from onboarding and ongoing support to managing renewals, price increases, and negotiations—even designing a Contract Generator to significantly speed up the process.
